E-Commerce Analytics

E-Commerce Industry Overview

  • What is E-Commerce?

    Business model enabling a firm or individual to conduct business over an electronic network, typically the internet.

  • Types of E-Commerce:

    • Consumer-to-Consumer (C2C): Ebay/Amazon, Airbnb, Uber/Didi, T-mall/Taobao,
    • Business-to-Consumer (B2C): Amazon.com, Walmart/Target.com, Alibaba/JD
    • Business-to-Business (B2B): Amazon AWS, MS Azure, Google Analytics, Aliyun.com, Square
    • Consumer-to-Business (C2B): Google Adsense, Survey Monkey

  • Product: physical/digital/service and etc.

Key Metrics in E-Commerce Industry

  • Traffic:

    • Visits - impression, click: repeat/new visitor by session/browser
    • Rates - click through rate (CTR), conversion rate

  • Sales/Transaction:

    • Gross: GsSales (FP/MD), GM%
    • Net: NetSales, NetGM%
    • Rates: RetRate, InvST

  • Customers:

    • New/Active Customers
    • Retention
    • Net Promoter Score (NPS)

  • Marketing:

    • CPA (CPC/PLA)
    • Est.Marketing Spend
      • Paid Search & SEO (Search Engine Optimization)
      • Retargeting
      • Affiliate
      • Social Media
      • CSE
      • Email

Digital Marketing Analytics Full Cycle

Oversee every process in typical E-Commerce to look out for potential issues, problems and potential for optimization to boost business performance.

General steps:

  1. Setup solution for request: understand business background, scoping for phase and ETA
  2. Data mining: collection/cleaning/massage
  3. Analytical analysis: A/B testing, regression modeling, seasonality/trending, etc.
  4. Summarize findings and insights: provide solution and/or recommendation

How to build customer profile?

  • Email Activity
    • open/click count and rate click
    • opt in/unsubscribe
  • Order Activity
    • product type
    • order date
    • order value
  • Demographic Information
    • gender
    • age
    • income
    • occupation/niches
    • region
    • credit/profitable_index
